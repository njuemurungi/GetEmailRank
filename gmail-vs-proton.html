Gmail vs Proton Mail: convenience vs. privacy sovereignty.

Gmail is the better default for most people. Proton Mail is the better choice when privacy, encryption, and independence matter more than maximum convenience.

Choose Gmail if...

  • You want the best spam filtering and search.
  • You use Google Calendar, Drive, Docs, or Android.
  • You want strong AI features and smart workflow tools.
  • You prefer convenience over maximum privacy.

Choose Proton Mail if...

  • You want strong encryption and privacy-first defaults.
  • You want to reduce dependence on Big Tech.
  • You are a journalist, activist, privacy-conscious professional, or security-sensitive user.
  • You can accept some workflow tradeoffs.

Verdict

Gmail wins for most people because it is faster, smarter, more integrated, and better at search and spam filtering. Proton Mail wins for people who see email as a private vault rather than a productivity layer.
