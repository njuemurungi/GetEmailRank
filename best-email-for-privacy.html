Privacy tradeoff

The more private a service is, the less it can usually do with AI, smart search, and automatic personalization. That is a feature, not necessarily a flaw.

Best normal-user pick

Proton Mail is the best privacy recommendation for most people because it combines strong privacy with enough polish and ecosystem maturity to be practical.
