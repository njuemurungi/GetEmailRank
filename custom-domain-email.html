What to look for

Good custom-domain support should include SPF, DKIM, DMARC guidance, aliases, easy DNS setup, catch-all options, migration tools, and clear admin controls.

Best overall pick

Fastmail is the strongest default because it treats custom domains as a core feature rather than an enterprise add-on.
